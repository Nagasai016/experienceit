Golconda Tour

Golconda was the principal capital of the Qutub Shahi kings. The inner fort contains ruins of palaces, mosques and a hill top pavilion, which rises about 130 meters high and gives a bird's eye view of other buildings. Golconda fort is undoubtedly one of most magnificent fortress complexes in India.

Price: ₹2,000

Charminar Tour

The Charminar is a mosque and monument located in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. Constructed in 1591, the landmark is a symbol of Hyderabad and officially incorporated in the emblem of Telangana The Charminar's long history includes the existence of a mosque on its top floor for more than 425 years

Price: ₹1,500

Salar Jung Museum Tour

The Salar Jung Museum is an art museum located at Dar-ul-Shifa, on the southern bank of the Musi River in the city of Hyderabad, Telangana, India. It is one of the notable National Museums of India.

Price: ₹2,500

Vagator Beach

Tourists and travel enthusiasts may have a once-in-a-lifetime experience at Vagator Beach in Goa. The sand and the water here are some of the most beautiful things you will see in Goa. Vagator beach has a magnificent view of the Arabian Sea and is one of the most popular beaches in Goa. The sunsets that you will witness here will be something to remember!

Price: ₹800

Dudhsagar Falls

The Dudhsagar Waterfalls, on the Goa-Karnataka border, is one of the most beautiful sceneries in Goa to quench your beauty thirst. On the Mandovi River, the four-tiered waterfall structure is one of the tallest in India and is a must-see attraction in India. From a height of nearly 1017 feet, the white water rushes down a virtually vertical mountain wall that looks magical.

Price: ₹3,000

Baga Beach

Baga Beach is well-known for its glitzy nightlife. Club Tito, Cafe Mambo, Capetown Cafe, Cocktails and Dreams, and Cavala are just a few of the numerous pubs, lounges, and clubs along Tito’s Lane, which help to make Baga Beach’s nightlife one of the greatest in Goa. Baga beach is also a popular hangout spot for nature lovers who want to see the scenery. A romantic Baga beach vacation begins with a candlelit supper on the beach for couples. Some additional exciting ventures, such as fishing, paddleboarding, discos, and tattoo parlors, make the place more interesting and attractive for tourists to visit.

Price: ₹1,000
